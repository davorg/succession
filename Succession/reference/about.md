# About This Site

This site tracks the British line of succession to the Crown. It shows who
would become monarch on any given date from 1820 to the present day.

## How It Works

Enter a date in the date picker to see the line of succession on that date.
You can also browse notable dates or see how the succession has changed over
time.

## Data Sources

The succession data is derived from public records, including Acts of
Parliament, Letters Patent, and official announcements.
